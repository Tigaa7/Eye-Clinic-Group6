Our Specialist Eye Care Services

Trusted diagnostics and personalised care for all your vision needs, under one roof.

🔬

Comprehensive Eye Examination

A full assessment of your visual health — checking acuity, eye muscle function, intraocular pressure, and retinal health using advanced diagnostic tools.

Visual Field Test

Precise mapping of your complete field of vision to detect and monitor conditions like glaucoma, optic nerve damage, and neurological disorders.
